# Catch the Stars

Guide a cute bunny to catch falling stars, hearts, moons, and diamonds!

## How to Play

1. **Move your mouse or finger** left and right to guide the bunny.
2. **Catch** stars, hearts, moons, and diamonds between the bunny's paws.
3. **Avoid** the gray rocks -- they cost you a life!
4. Build up a **combo** by catching items in a row without missing.
5. Each level makes things fall faster. How far can you go?
6. **Tap anywhere** to restart after game over.

## Controls

- **Mouse / Touch** -- move the bunny left and right

## Details

- 5 lives (shown as hearts)
- Speed increases with each level
- Combo counter tracks consecutive catches
- Beautiful night sky backdrop
